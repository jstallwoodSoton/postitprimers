Postit Primers: An Overview

Postit Primers are short, sticky note sized, and easy to digest primers of important information.

Color coded to point students to the appropriate level of information needed for any lesson, they facilitate directed learning using systems already familiar to most.

Postit Primers demand that instructors be concise and get their points across in as few words as possible.

However, they also have the benefit of being easy to reference when directing students' learning:


"This week's homework, familiarize yourselves with Eigenvalues Definition, and Eigenvalues Proofs 1-4."
