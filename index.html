Mappings & Functions

D1

\(f:S \rightarrow T\)

(mapping):(domain)\(\rightarrow\)(codomain)

D2

\((S\neq \varnothing \land T \neq \varnothing) \implies\)

\(f \subset S \times T:\forall s \in S, \exists!t \in T\)

\((s, t) \in f\)

E1

\(f:\mathbb{Q}_{>0}\rightarrow \mathbb{Z}\times\mathbb{Z}\)

\(s = \frac{m}{n}, t = (m, n) \iff s \mapsto t\)

D3

\(f:S\rightarrow T\) is one-to-one

\(s_{i} \neq s_{j} \iff f(s_{i}) \neq f(s_{j})\)

\(\forall s_{i}, s_{j} \in S\)

D4

\(f:S\rightarrow T \quad g:S\rightarrow T\)

\((\forall s \in S)[f(s) = g(s)] \implies f = g\)

D5

\(f:S\rightarrow T \quad g:T\rightarrow U\)

\(f\circ g:S \rightarrow U\)

\((f\circ g)(s) = g(f(s))\)

D6

\(f:S\rightarrow T \quad g:T\rightarrow U\)

\(h:U\rightarrow V\)

\((f\circ g)\circ h = f\circ(g\circ h)\)

D7

Let \(S \neq \varnothing\)

\(A(S) := \{f\mid f:S\rightarrow S\}\)

D8

Let \(\alpha, \beta, \gamma \in A(S)\):

  • \(\alpha \circ \beta \in A(S)\)
  • \((\alpha \circ \beta)\circ \gamma = \alpha \circ (\beta \circ \gamma)\)
  • \(\exists! \iota \in A(S): \alpha\circ\iota = \iota\circ\alpha = \alpha\)
  • \(\exists! \alpha^{-1} \in A(S): \alpha\circ\alpha^{-1} = \alpha^{-1}\circ\alpha = \iota\)

D9

Let \(f:\mathbb{R}^{n}\rightarrow \mathbb{R}\)

Where \(n \in \mathbb{Z}_{>0}\)

Let \(x_{1}, x_{2} \in \mathbb{R}^{n}: x_{1} \neq x_{2}\)

Let \(\lambda \in [0, 1]\)

D10

\(f\) is convex iff:

\(f(\lambda x_{1} + (1 - \lambda)x_{2}) \le\)

\(\lambda f(x_{1}) + (1 - \lambda)f(x_{2})\)

\(\forall x_{1}, x_{2} \in \mathbb{R}^{n}\)
